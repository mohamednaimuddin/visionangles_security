Video Intercom Solutions

Advanced video intercoms with smartphone access and integrated door locks. Dahua professional systems for apartment buildings, offices, and commercial facilities. Cloud-enabled remote communication with visitor verification and door unlock capabilities installed nationwide.

Our Partners

Video intercom systems provide secure visitor screening with high-definition video and audio communication. Our range includes residential apartment systems, commercial office intercoms, and industrial emergency stations. Features include remote door release integration, call recording, mobile app access, emergency buttons, and integration with access control systems. Dahua and Hikvision systems offer IP-based connectivity with cloud management, enabling remote communication from anywhere in Saudi Arabia and worldwide.
