Editorial Policy

Commercial and technical content is reviewed by the Visionangles Security technical sales and ELV integration team before publication or major revision. Pages are reviewed when product lines change, manufacturers publish important updates, or service coverage changes.

Product descriptions are based on manufacturer documentation, project experience, internal engineering practice, and market availability at the time of review.

Website content is informational and does not replace a site survey, compliance assessment, or formal engineering design. Final recommendations depend on local conditions, building requirements, risk profile, and approved project scope.
