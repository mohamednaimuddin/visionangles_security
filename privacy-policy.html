Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Visionangles Security collects, uses, and protects information submitted through this website, including contact enquiries, quotation requests, and support messages.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, company name, email address, phone number, branch selection, project details, and any message you submit through our forms or by direct email and phone contact.

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to enquiries, prepare quotations, schedule site surveys, deliver after-sales support, and improve our customer service and website experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared with service providers involved in processing website enquiries or supporting project delivery, only where necessary for legitimate business purposes.

Retention and Security

We retain enquiry records for as long as reasonably necessary for business operations, compliance, dispute resolution, and customer support. We apply administrative and technical safeguards appropriate to the information we process.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request correction or deletion of personal information that is no longer needed, subject to contractual, legal, and operational requirements.
