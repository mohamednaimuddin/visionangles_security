Frequently Asked

Answers Before You Ask

Quick facts about Vision Angles Security — what we do, where we work, and how to reach us.

What services does Vision Angles Security provide in Saudi Arabia?

Vision Angles Security is a full-service ELV and security systems integrator in Saudi Arabia. We supply, install and maintain CCTV and IP surveillance, access control, video intercom, burglar alarms, network infrastructure, public-address audio, thermal cameras, LED displays, speed gates, interactive flat panels and parking management systems. We serve homes, retail shops, offices, schools and industrial sites across Al Khobar, Al Jubail, Dammam, Riyadh and Jeddah.

Where is Vision Angles Security located?

Our head office is in Al Khobar (postal code 34714), Eastern Province, Saudi Arabia. We operate two showrooms — Al Khobar and Al Jubail — and two regional offices in Riyadh (Central Region) and Jeddah (Western Region).

Which security brands does Vision Angles Security install?

We are an authorized integrator and partner for Hikvision, Dahua, ZKTeco, EZVIZ, Bosch, Hanwha, Paradox and Ajax for surveillance and alarms; Sophos, Fortinet, WatchGuard, Cisco, TP-Link, D-Link and Ruijie for network and security infrastructure; Grandstream and Cisco for IP telephony; Bose, Ahuja, Shure and ProSound for audio; and Lampro, Samsung and partner brands for LED displays and interactive panels.

Do you provide installation and after-sales support?

Yes. Every project includes site survey, design, supply, installation, commissioning, user training and ongoing maintenance. Our local technicians cover Al Khobar, Al Jubail, Dammam, Riyadh and Jeddah, and we offer annual maintenance contracts (AMC) with same-day response in the Eastern Province.

How do I request a quote from Vision Angles Security?

You can request a quote by calling +966 13 822 6660, sending WhatsApp to +966 54 165 2927, emailing info@visionangles.com, or submitting the contact form at visionangles.com/contact. Quotes are typically returned within one business day.

What are Vision Angles Security's working hours?

Our head office in Al Khobar operates Saturday to Thursday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. The Al Khobar and Al Jubail showrooms are open Saturday to Thursday, 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM. We are closed on Fridays.
